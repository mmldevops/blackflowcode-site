Direct answers to the questions we hear most often on intro calls and that answer engines surface for queries about AI automation agencies and Salesforce Agentforce.

What is Blackflowcode.ai?
Blackflowcode.ai is an AI workflow automation agency that deploys Salesforce Agentforce agents, CRM revenue operations systems on HubSpot and Salesforce, voice AI agents, and custom workflow automation for B2B SMB and mid-market teams across the United States.
Who founded Blackflowcode.ai?
Blackflowcode.ai was founded by Neno Gaspard, a senior enterprise architect with over 10 years of experience at TD Bank, Wells Fargo, and in property and casualty insurance deployments, and healthcare trust onboarding for medical building acquisitions.
How does the engagement timeline work?
Every engagement is discovery based. After the intro call, a fixed scope proposal is delivered within 3–5 business days. Most engagements complete within 30–120 days from signed SOW to production go-live. Every engagement includes 30 days of post-launch hypercare.
How does pricing work?
All engagements are fixed scope and fixed price. Pricing is provided in writing during the proposal phase, after scope is confirmed on the intro call. No hourly billing. No time and materials contracts.
What industries does Blackflowcode.ai serve?
Direct experience across banking, insurance, healthcare, legal, home services, dental, and B2B SaaS. Regulated industry security posture is included in every engagement by default.
